New Publishing

Experience a collection of soul-refreshing poems in "44 Christian Rhyme Poems." These inspirational verses are suitable for readers of all ages. Accompanied by captivating photos, this book invites you to embark on an imaginative journey. Let the simple yet profound words within nourish your spirit and uplift your soul.

In the secret place of my soul we meet Lean on His chest and listen for the beats He utters many things that are deep In His mouth there is no deceit
-- Make the Leap

In all your trials I remain faithful and true From deep waters I stretched out and drew Through the fire and testing you grew I gave you wings you rised up and flew
-- Faithful and True

Follow the guidance of Divine intuition Embark unto a journey with a new sense of mission Strengthen us through all the phases of transitions Strife to live a life of surrender and submission
-- Valley of Decision
