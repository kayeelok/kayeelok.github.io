Featured Books

The Archer is a collection of 44 bible teachings on a variety of topics complied from previous online posts.

Yarah יָרָה in Hebrew means “to shoot, to teach”. Interesting and inspirational, the author “shot at” etymology, history, politics, mythology, astrology, science, cultures, religions, philosophies, etc. Every chapter is an adventure with God that she invites you to join. You will gain better understanding of and find encouragement in His words.
