New Publishing

Experience a collection of soul-refreshing poems in "44 Christian Rhyme Poems." These inspirational verses are suitable for readers of all ages. Accompanied by captivating photos, this book invites you to embark on an imaginative journey. Let the simple yet profound words within nourish your spirit and uplift your soul.

I hear the sound of an abundance of rain I wait for my turn to hop into the Destiny train I stretch beyond what I can conceive with my brain I reach out to Your Promises where I can attain -- Abundance of Rain

In the secret place of thunder I call You in trouble and You deliver You tested us at the waters of Meribah You reveal secrets of the hearts in prayer -- Touch This Sinner

You made the Pleiades and Orion The Sun and the Moon at the horizon I hear a clarion call from Mount Zion My spiritual senses become heightened -- Pleiades and Orion

I come before You in my silent prayer I look attentively to the empty chair Do angels ascend and descend these stairs Secretly I long to see my missing hare -- Silent Prayer
