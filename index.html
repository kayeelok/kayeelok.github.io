Sweet Fragrance

I take a costly oil of sweet fragrance

I anoint His feet with extravagance

Wipe with my hair with obeisance

The house is filled with aroma at once

I learn at Your feet with long patience

Precept upon precept is the essence

Wrap around me with Your presence

You are as my balm, oil and redolence

Fill this night with music and pleasance

I rejoice greatly at Your appearance
