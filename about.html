About the author

Ka Yee Lok, originally from Hong Kong, immigrated to Canada at a young age. Raised in a Catholic family, she later embraced Pentecostalism after having encountered a Throne vision of God. Ka Yee holds a Bachelor's degree in Biology, a M.Sc. in Clinical Trials, and a graduate diploma in Aviation Management. Currently, she is pursuing degrees in Theology and learning Hebrew. Her deep passion lies in hearing God's voice and sharing about Christ and His Word with fervor.

She finds joy in reading, writing, music, visual arts, sewing, rabbits and hiking. Ka Yee resides in Brossard, Quebec, Canada, where she is learning the skills of writing diversely. For updates and more, follow her on Facebook at the account kayee.lok444.
